About FreeAudit

Service Order Audit Platform · Freedom Logistics Service Solutions

Auditing service orders by hand is slow and easy to get wrong — opening every order in Fullbay, eyeballing photos, checking parts, hunting for missing paperwork. FreeAudit was built to streamline that entire process and make great use of your time: it turns a tedious, click-through-every-order review into a one-click pass, then hands you a clean, prioritized list of exactly what needs fixing — so you can focus on the work that actually moves the shop forward.

With one click it signs into Fullbay, finds every order at Ready to Invoice, opens each one, and runs a consistent set of checks — bundling the photos, technicians, and notes into a single report. On every order it verifies:

What it checks
  • Photos — repair work has before and after photos
  • Parts — repair labor actually has parts on it
  • Inspections — DOT/PM complaints have paperwork attached
  • Hours — no service stuck at 0.00 invoiced hours
  • PO numbers — valid MT- format, not malformed or duplicated
  • Duplicate photos — same photo not reused across orders
  • Completion tracker — unit marked complete in the Google Sheet
Why it's powerful for admin & management
  • Standards enforced before money goes out — problems caught before invoicing
  • Accountability — findings tied to a technician; reused-photo detection surfaces corner-cutting
  • Less missed revenue & rework — missing parts, hours, and paperwork flagged automatically
  • Faster close-out — hours of manual review become a few-minute automated pass
  • Oversight from anywhere — review results securely, even off-site

How it works

Three steps from login to a finished audit.

1
Sign in to Fullbay
Click Sign in to Fullbay once on the shop computer (and again only if the session times out). No passwords are stored.
2
Run the audit
Click Run Audit. FreeAudit opens every Ready-to-Invoice order and checks it automatically — photos, parts, inspections, hours, POs, duplicate photos, and the completion tracker.
3
Review the report
In a few minutes you get a clean, prioritized report — with photos, technicians, and notes attached — plus a CSV. Fix what's flagged, then invoice with confidence.
